# DevFlow - Innovation Operating System Overview

DevFlow is a premium, multi-agent AI-powered innovation operating system designed to guide students, startups, educational groups, and NGOs from initial concept discovery to presentation-ready project formulations.

## 🎯 Target Solve for Tomorrow Objectives

- **Structured Methodology**: Incorporates Samsung Solve for Tomorrow (SFT) & Design Thinking milestones (Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype, Test).
- **Assumptions Validation**: Employs deep user research observation logs and 5 Whys templates.
- **SDG Mappings**: Integrates direct linkages to the 17 UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) with contribution weights and outcome predictions.
- **Advisory Council**: Multi-agent consensus reviews modeling technical, sustainability, UX, strategical, ethical, and presentation-ready judge criteria.

## 🧭 Dashboard Modules

1.  **Innovation Workspace**: Master sprint dashboards hosting active projects, health indices, and design thinking timeline wizards.
2.  **Problem Discovery**: Deconstructs initial user problem assumptions through stakeholder maps, Chronological Research logs, and visual Fishbone skeletons.
3.  **Impact Intelligence**: Maps UN SDGs, estimates reach ratios, outlines risk mitigation structures, and measures inclusivity scales.
4.  **AI Innovation Council**: Aggregates multi-agent feedback from eight specialized advisors to form consolidated reports and pitch presentations.
